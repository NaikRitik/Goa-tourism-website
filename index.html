About Goa


Dudhsagar Falls


Discover the majestic Dudhsagar Waterfalls cascading down the lush forests of the Western Ghats. Standing at over 300 meters tall, it's one of India's tallest waterfalls and a must-see natural wonder. Reach the falls by an exciting jeep safari through the Bhagwan Mahavir Wildlife Sanctuary, and enjoy stunning views, misty spray, and sometimes even a glimpse of the passing railway bridge above.

Miramar Beach


Relax and unwind at Miramar Beach, one of Goa's most popular urban beaches just 3 km from Panjim city. This golden stretch of sand is perfect for evening walks, playing beach games, or watching a breathtaking sunset as the Mandovi River meets the Arabian Sea. With nearby cafes and street food stalls, it's a favorite spot for locals and tourists alike.

Old Goa Church


Step back in time and explore the UNESCO World Heritage churches of Old Goa, once the capital of Portuguese India. Visit the Basilica of Bom Jesus, which houses the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier, and admire grand baroque architecture, intricate altars, and centuries-old art. This historic site reflects Goa's rich colonial past and attracts pilgrims and history lovers from around the world.
